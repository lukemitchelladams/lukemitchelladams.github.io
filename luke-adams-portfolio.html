About Me
From IT Operations to Cloud Architecture

I'm Luke Adams — an IT professional based in the Denver/Front Range area transitioning into cloud solutions architecture. With 7+ years managing IT operations, security tooling, and identity systems for small-to-mid-size businesses, I bring a practitioner's understanding of what makes infrastructure reliable, secure, and maintainable.

What sets me apart: I don't just study — I build. Every week brings new projects, new labs, and deeper understanding of cloud-native design patterns. I'm looking for a team that values curiosity, follow-through, and a bias toward action.
